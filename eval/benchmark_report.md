**Manual Testing Failure Modes**
1. **Backlighting/Low Light:** MediaPipe holistic detection degrades rapidly in uneven lighting, causing the valid frame mask to drop below the threshold.
2. **Speed Blurring:** Fast signing results in motion blur, occasionally causing hand landmark inversion.
3. **Out-of-Frame Articulation:** Signs that require extending arms far horizontally may exit the standard webcam field of view.

## 5. Known Limitations

- **Dataset Size Limits:** INCLUDE-50 and INCLUDE are fundamentally small-scale datasets compared to spoken language corpora. Generalization to unseen vocabularies is zero.
- **Signer Diversity & Overfitting:** Even with signer-independent splits, the total pool of 115 signers (in the full dataset) lacks representation of broad regional ISL dialects.
- **Regional Dialect Gaps:** The system is trained on standardized ISL (predominantly from the Mumbai/Delhi region dataset contributors) and will likely fail on regional variants.
- **Generative Synthesis Gap:** Text-to-ISL is implemented as a lookup-and-stitch retrieval mechanism, not true generative synthesis, making it brittle to out-of-vocabulary words.
